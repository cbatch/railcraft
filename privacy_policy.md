# Railcraft Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Railcraft mobile application.

## Data Collection

Railcraft does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.
All gameplay data is stored locally on the user's device.

## In-App Purchases

Railcraft uses Apple's in-app purchase system. Purchase information is handled entirely by Apple and is not accessible to the developer.

## Third-Party Services

Railcraft does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Railcraft does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
